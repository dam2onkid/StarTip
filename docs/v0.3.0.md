# StarTip v0.3.0 Overview

Last updated: 2026-07-21

## Goal

v0.3.0 is the seven-day hackathon release that proves StarTip can make a
stablecoin donation feel as simple as a familiar consumer payment. A donor uses
a passkey-backed Stellar C-account, the Sponsor covers the network fee, the
DonationRouter settles the payment, and the Creator sees the result on the OBS
overlay.

The release is successful when this complete flow works repeatedly on Stellar
testnet and can be demonstrated with real on-chain evidence.

## Scope

- One Creator, one QR, one test asset, and one primary donation flow.
- A real passkey controls a real Stellar C-account.
- The C-account holds and donates a Stellar Asset Contract asset.
- A dedicated Sponsor pays transaction fees without exposing its secret.
- DonationRouter splits the payment and emits the donor identity.
- The existing indexer and OBS overlay display the donation exactly once.

Mainnet, fiat integration, account recovery, multiple assets, swaps, bridges,
and a public SDK remain outside this release.

## Starting point

StarTip already has a working web MVP, DonationRouter, donation indexer,
Creator dashboard, and OBS overlay. The passkey wallet boundary, sponsored
transaction routes, prepared authorization, replay protection, rate limiting,
and donor event identity are also part of the current foundation.

The remaining priority is to connect these parts into one reliable testnet
demonstration and prepare clear evidence for the judges.

## 1 - Testnet and asset foundation

Prepare the Sponsor account, one test asset, its Stellar Asset Contract, and the
DonationRouter allowlist. By the end of the day, the environment should be
reproducible and ready to fund a C-account.

Relevant Stellar references:

- [Deploy to Testnet](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/build/smart-contracts/getting-started/deploy-to-testnet)
- [Stellar CLI](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/tools/cli/stellar-cli)
- [Issue an asset](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/tokens/how-to-issue-an-asset)
- [Stellar Asset Contract](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/tokens/stellar-asset-contract)

## 2 - Passkey C-account

Create a real passkey-backed account, bind it to the donor, and fund it with the
test asset. The main result is a verified C-account whose signer and asset
balance can be read from testnet.

Relevant Stellar references:

- [Contract accounts](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/build/guides/contract-accounts)
- [Smart wallets and passkeys](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/build/guides/contract-accounts/smart-wallets)
- [Contract authorization](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/build/guides/auth/contract-authorization)
- [Payments with contract accounts](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/build/guides/transactions/send-and-receive-c-accounts)

## 3 - Sponsored donation

Complete passkey authorization for `DonationRouter.donate`, let the Sponsor
assemble and submit the transaction, and confirm a successful donation on
testnet. The Sponsor pays the fee while the C-account remains the authorized
donor.

Relevant Stellar references:

- [Signing Soroban invocations](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/build/guides/transactions/signing-soroban-invocations)
- `[simulateTransaction](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/data/apis/rpc/api-reference/methods/simulateTransaction)`
- `[sendTransaction](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/data/apis/rpc/api-reference/methods/sendTransaction)`
- `[getTransaction](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/data/apis/rpc/api-reference/methods/getTransaction)`

## 4 - Complete donation experience

Connect the public QR flow to the sponsored donation and then to the existing
indexer and OBS overlay. The result should be one continuous journey from QR to
on-chain settlement to a single visible Creator alert.

Relevant Stellar references:

- [Contract events](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/build/guides/events)
- `[getEvents](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/data/apis/rpc/api-reference/methods/getEvents)`
- [Event ingestion](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/build/guides/events/ingest)

## 5 - Security and reliability

Verify that replayed, modified, expired, unauthorized, and excessive requests
are rejected. Confirm that RPC delays and failed submissions do not create
duplicate donations or misleading states.

Relevant Stellar references:

- [Contract authorization](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/build/guides/auth/contract-authorization)
- [Contract testing](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/build/guides/testing)
- [Stellar security guidance](https://developers.stellar.org/docs/build/security-docs)

## Expected result

- A real passkey-backed C-account donates one SAC asset on testnet.
- The Sponsor pays the network fee without taking custody of donor funds.
- DonationRouter distributes the payment atomically.
- The C-account is preserved as the donor identity.
- The donation appears exactly once in the indexer and OBS overlay.
- The team has a repeatable live demo and verifiable on-chain evidence.

## After v0.3.0

The next stage should focus on recovery, production sponsorship policy,
contract review, reconciliation, and a small Creator pilot. Mainnet expansion,
regional stablecoins, fiat partners, and third-party integrations should only
follow after the core donation flow is reliable.
